**Company Overview:**
Daqo New Energy Corp. is a leading manufacturer of high-purity polysilicon for the solar photovoltaic industry. The company is positioned within the semiconductor equipment and materials industry, providing essential materials for the production of solar panels. Daqo has shown solid revenue growth in recent years, driven by the increasing global demand for renewable energy solutions. However, the company has faced challenges due to volatile market conditions and pricing pressures in the solar industry.

**Strengths & Weaknesses:**
One of Daqo's key strengths is its leading position in the polysilicon market, with a reputation for high-quality products. The company benefits from the growing demand for solar energy solutions and the shift towards renewable sources of power. However, Daqo also faces risks related to fluctuating raw material prices, competition from other polysilicon manufacturers, and regulatory uncertainties in the renewable energy sector. Additionally, the company's negative free cash flow and net income raise concerns about its financial stability.
